Chuka University is proud to announce the launch of its new security features aimed at ensuring the safety of its students and staff. In light of recent security concerns on campuses across the country, the Campus has taken proactive steps to enhance its security measures. The new security features include: New security cameras installed throughout campus: The new cameras are equipped with state-of-the-art technology that will allow campus security to monitor all areas of campus 24/7. Increased security patrols by campus police: Campus police will be patrolling campus more frequently, both on foot and in vehicles. New emergency call boxes installed in key locations: The new emergency call boxes will allow students and staff to quickly and easily contact campus security in the event of an emergency. Improved lighting in parking areas and walkways: The improved lighting will make it easier to see in these areas, which will help to deter crime. New security gates installed at all entrances to campus: The new security gates will be staffed by security guards who will check the identification of all visitors to campus. In addition to the new security features, Chuka University is also educating its students and staff about safety and security best practices. The University offers a variety of safety workshops and training programs, and it encourages students and staff to report any suspicious activity to campus security. Chuka University believes that the new security features and safety programs will help to create a safe and welcoming community for all. The University is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for its students and staff, and it is confident that these measures will help to deter crime and keep its community safe.
